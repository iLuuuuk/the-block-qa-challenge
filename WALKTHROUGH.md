# The Walkthrough

After submission, we schedule a **45-60 minute conversation**. You will screen-share and walk us through your QA automation work.

## What to Expect

### 1. Demo (~10 min)

Show your automation running and walk through:

- what you covered
- what you intentionally did not cover
- how to interpret results

### 2. Strategy & Product Risk (~15 min)

We will discuss:

- how you prioritized scenarios
- what risks you targeted first
- tradeoffs made inside the timebox

### 3. Technical Deep-Dive (~15 min)

We will inspect:

- test architecture and abstractions
- data setup and isolation approach
- flake mitigation and debugging strategy
- API vs E2E balance

### 4. Workflow & AI (~15 min)

We will discuss:

- how you used AI tools (if any)
- where AI helped and where it needed correction
- how you validated generated outputs
- how you maintained confidence in your suite

### 5. Questions (~5 min)

Your turn to ask about the team, role, or product.

## How to Prepare

- Be ready to run key tests live
- Know your code and decisions end-to-end
- Be explicit about assumptions and gaps
- Be ready to discuss reliability and maintenance implications
